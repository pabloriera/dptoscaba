Buenos Aires housing snapshot

See how asking rents vary across neighborhoods

This page turns current apartment listings into a visual guide for people who want a quick feel for the market. It is meant to be easy to read: where prices tend to sit, how wide the spread is, and which areas stand apart.

At a glance

How to read the charts

Each neighborhood appears as a horizontal boxplot. The middle line shows a typical asking rent, the box shows the main range, and the whiskers show how far prices stretch beyond that core band.

The result is useful for quick comparisons: where rents cluster tightly, where they spread widely, and where premium areas pull away from the rest.

What this is for

Use it to explore neighborhoods, compare general price levels, and understand the size of the gap between more accessible and more expensive areas.

Important note

These are asking prices from listings, not final contract values. Individual homes can still differ a lot depending on size, condition, building amenities, and exact location.
